Skillet Lasagna

Description

This simple and quick no-bake skillet lasagna is a great option if you're sick of those store-bought frozen lasagnas

Preparation time:10 minutes

Cook time:35 minutes

Total time:45 minutes

Serves:3

Ingredients

  • Half a pound of ground beef
  • 14 ounces of your favorite spaghetti sauce
  • 14.5 ounces of can diced tomatoes
  • half an onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil (Optional)
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano (Optional)
