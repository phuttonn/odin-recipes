Spicy Peruvian Chicken

Description

This Delicious chicken dish will have everyone asking for seconds! Chicken thighs roasted to perfection topped with spicy jalapeno-cilantro-feta salsa.

Cook time: 40 minutes

Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 small bunches of cilantro
  • 4 ounces of feta cheese, crumbled
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 jalapeno peppers
  • Black pepper
  • Brown sugar
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Cumin spice
  • Extra virgin olive olive
  • oregano, dried
  • Salt
  • 2 pounds of sweet potatoes
  • 2 limes

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Position racks in the upper and lower thirds of the oven. Line a large baking sheet pan with aluminum foil.
  2. Wash and dry the fresh produce.
    • jalapenos
    • cilantro
    • limes
    • sweet potatoes
  3. Peel and slice sweet potatoes into ½-inch thick rounds. Place in a large bowl, drizzle with oil, and season with salt and pepper. Toss to coat.
    • 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil
    • quarter tsp salt
    • quarter tsp black pepper
  4. Arrange sweet potatoes on the baking sheet in a single layer. Place on the lower rack of the oven and bake, flipping halfway through, until fork-tender, about 25 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, zest and juice one lime into the bowl used for the sweet potatoes. Set remaining lime aside.
  6. Peel and mince garlic. Add to the bowl along with oil, sugar, and spices. Stir to combine the spice paste.
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 2 tsp extra virgin olive oil
    • one and a half tsp brown sugar
    • one and a half tsp cumin
    • half tsp oregano
    • half tsp cayenne pepper
    • half tsp salt
  7. Preheat a large ovenproof skillet over medium-high heat.
  8. While the skillet heats up, pat chicken thighs dry with paper towels and add to the bowl with the spice paste; turn to coat.
  9. Once the skillet is hot, add oil and swirl to coat the bottom. Add chicken in a single layer, and cook on one side until golden brown and caramelized, about 5 minutes.
  10. Flip and cook for 1 minute more, then remove from heat (the chicken will not be cooked all the way through).
  11. Transfer the skillet to the upper rack of the oven and roast until chicken is cooked through, 12-15 minutes.
  12. Meanwhile, juice remaining lime into a medium bowl
  13. Quarter jalapeño peppers lengthwise; seed and remove ribs with a spoon. Mince and add to the bowl with the lime juice. Caution around your eyes when handling jalapenos!
  14. Shave cilantro leaves off the stems; discard stems and mince the leaves. Add to the bowl.
  15. Peel and mince additional garlic. Add to the bowl along with feta, oil, sugar, cumin, and salt. Stir to combine the salsa.
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 4 ounce of feta
    • 4 tsp extra virgin olive oil
    • 1 tsp brown sugar
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • quarter tsp salt
  16. To serve, divide sweet potatoes and chicken between plates. Spoon salsa over chicken and enjoy!
